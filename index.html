Snap-Draw Holsters

Scroll down for more info

Our product was inspired by the recent wave of backlash on our police departments in America, as well as a recent news story about a deputy pulling a gun while off duty. We wanted to help reduce tensions and make it easier to provide evidence in court. In the case where body cameras or security cameras are not functioning properly or present, we need a tamper-proof way to tell what exactly happened in the heat of the situation. That's why we came up with the Snap-Draw holsters: a cheap and easy method to keeping track of a firefight. This easy and ready to produce device can send Bluetooth signals about when the gun has been removed and put back in it's holsters. Once fully developed, it could be used for shots fired, and safety turned off. Keep in mind that although the device is designed for police security, we support our deputies and wish them luck in their careers. This is for everyone's safety, including theirs. Feel free to read through the rest of the website.

The way our product works is whenever you pull out your gun, turn of the safety, or fire a bullet, it will track the amount of times you have done so.
